About me

I've found joy in coding but music has always been one of my passions. Some people may think it's hard to reconcile the worlds of programming and art but I personally believe the composer and the developer share a multitude of the same practices. Both professions require a high degree creativity and focus, coding is more rigid obviously but crafting a coherent piece of music does utilize the same big picture mindset needed to say, develop a new app or algorithim. Hopefully you can see that my background in art gives me a unique perspective for whatever task at hand.
